### After
- → use parametric bootstrap!
	- 1. Estimate $\hat\theta$ by MM.
	- 2. Compute $\hat\Lambda (\hat\theta)$ by test2.
	- 3. Generate $M=1000$ samples from $F_{\hat\theta}$ with no bias.
	- 4. For each sample, compute $\hat\Lambda_i(\hat\theta)$ by test2 (no need to estimate $\hat\theta$ again).
	- 5. The p-value $=\min(\frac{1}{M}\sum I_{\{\hat\Lambda_i(\hat\theta)>\hat\Lambda (\hat\theta)\}},\frac{1}{M}\sum I_{\{\hat\Lambda_i(\hat\theta)<\hat\Lambda (\hat\theta)\}})=0.003$.
